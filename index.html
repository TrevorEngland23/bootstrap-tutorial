Inside the book...

A quick glance at the topics you'll learn

Are you ready to design your webpage? You've come to the right place. In this chapter, we will discuss all the HTML basics to get your webpage up and running!

Topics include: HTML tags, attributes, linking external files, inserting images, the importance of id's and classes, and much more!

Wouldn't it be nice to know how to make your webpage look aesthetic? Look no further, this chapter covers everything you need to know to make that webpage pop!

Topics include: CSS formatting, attributes and values, web-design using CSS, tables, grid, flexbox, and more!

Getting started with JavaSript doesn't have to be difficult... Net Ninja Pro paves the road for you, all you have to do is follow!

Topics include: JavaScript history, what is JavaScript, why do we use JavaScript, and why you should learn.

Grab a seat, open your computer, and code away! This chapter starts your long journey into learning this programming language.

This chapter includes: multiple detailed examples, practice coding challenges with provided correct answers, and making your first program with JavaScript!

Imagine having a calendar in every room of your house, and any time you needed to update your calendar, you had to update every calendar in your house. If only there was a better way to make this more... functional...

Introducing functions! In this chapter you'll learn the importance of using functions in programming, in addition to parameters, arguments, higher-order functions, etc!
